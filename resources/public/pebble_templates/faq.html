{{i18n('Localization', 'Why is the cooldown as long as it is?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'The time it takes to get a pixel is dynamic, and changes based on how many people are online. The cooldown time is longer when more users are online.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Why does it say 0/6 pixels?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'This means that you are waiting for your next pixel. When you place a pixel, there is a cooldown for when you can place the next one. Over time, you can gain up to 6 "stacked" pixels to place at once.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Why does it take so long to "stack" pixels?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'This is by design. It is better to place a pixel as soon as you get one. Pixel "stacking" is meant to give you a bump if you go AFK for a bit. It takes around 40 minutes to get a full 6/6 pixels, but it only takes around 3 minutes to place 6 pixels if you place them as soon as possible.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'How do I appeal a ban?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Contact us on Discord or send an appeal to our Google form after reading the rules in the site\'s info panel.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'How do I make a template?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'A template is an image link that is posted in the template textbox in the settings panel so you can place over it on the canvas. You may use a regular image link to your pixel art, but many people use the 3rd party tool PxlsFiddle to turn pixel art into a fancier link. If you need to make pixel art yourself, you can use Piskel to create art to put into PxlsFiddle.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'How do I move a template?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Hold CTRL (or OPTION on macOS) and click and drag to move it around. On mobile, you can tap and hold where you want to move the template and click "Move Template Here" in the pop-up.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Does the canvas reset? When?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Yes. The canvas resets when it is completely full. A reset date is not usually decided until the canvas is full, but the average canvas life-span is around 1 month. Updates are posted in our Discord server when a reset date is set.') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'Where can I see the past canvases?') | raw}}
{{i18n('Localization', 'The past canvases are featured on our Discord server, or the 3rd party website PxlsFiddle, which records timelapses and a lot of useful information on each canvas.') | raw}}
